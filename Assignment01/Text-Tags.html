Famous Proverb

A bird is known by its note, and a man by his talk.
Ray, John. A Collection of English Proverbs. 1670. Bartleby.com, The Proverb's Citation Link.

The above proverb has many variation across various cultures, and one of the recent variation being: 
A bird is known by its song, and a man by his words
.

This proverb carries the profound meaning of Just as you can recognize a bird by the way it sings, you can recognize a person’s character by the way they speak.
